What is Meltomo?

　Stay curious and connect　

Meltomo is an online platform created by three Japanese exchange students in Melbourne. The website connects Australian and Japanese culture in Melbourne, and allows its users to get in touch with various aspects of the beautiful city - the people and culture of Melbourne are accessible via our platform with one single click. Not only does Meltomo provide information about Melbourne and Japan, but it also provides its users with the opportunity to interact with each other, creating a more personal experience for each member of the Meltomo community. You too can join the community today and expand your horizons - the opportunities really are endless.

Japanese Urban Dictionary

"I understand what's going on in Japanese class, but when I try and have a casual conversation with my Japanese friends I just can't do it.." Is this you? We believe the problem is 'Young people language'. Yes, young people language! It is a thing! The slang that young, native Japanese people use is almost impossible to learn from a textbook in class. This is why we have created our very own original 'Japanese Urban Dictionary'. In this dictionary we have collected various popular slang that 'young Japanese people' use on a day to day basis! We have combined memorable graphics with straight forward example sentences to make it easy for you guys to learn from and remember. So why not give it a try today? Check out our Japanese Urban Dictionary and impress your Japanese friends with your skills
